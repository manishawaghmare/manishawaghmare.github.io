Hello, I am Manisha Waghamare...

I am an Indian Mountaineer. I have climbed highest mountains in Europe, Africa and Australia continents. I have been working hard for climbing highest peak on Earth- Mount Everest (29035 feet). Since last 10 years I have done several mountaineering courses and participated in various adventures activities like SKY DIVING and High Altitude expeditions.

Currently I am working as 'Physical education director' in Dr. (Sow) Indirabai BHaskarrao Pathak Mahila Mahavidyalaya, Aurangabad.
